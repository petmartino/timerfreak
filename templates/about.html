About TimerFreak

TimerFreak is a simple yet powerful web application designed to help you manage timed sequences. Whether you're following a workout routine, deep work session, a cooking recipe, or any activity that requires a series of timed steps, TimerFreak ensures you stay on track with visual progress bars and custom alarms for each segment.

Easily create custom sequences with multiple timers, customize according to your needs, It's built to be straightforward and effective, helping you focus on the task at hand without distractions.

Code at https://github.com/petmartino/timerfreak
